Early History

While the stories of the founding of Jeffrey's Bay range from traders opening shop, to lost captains building home's from their shipwrecks, to whalers settling down, it is generally accepted that Jeffrey's bay traces it origins to the year 1849. The same year that nearby Humansdorp was founded. Jeffrey's bay grew from a small settlement into a exceedingly popular holiday destination.
